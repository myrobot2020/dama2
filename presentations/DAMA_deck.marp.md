---

## What DAMA Is

DAMA is **not** a chatbot or content app.

It is a **strict verification + gradual teaching system** that:

- Maps your current mind-state to the correct entry point in the canon
- Uses a clear 5-level structure:
  - **Level 1**: Canon from trusted transcripts
  - **Level 2**: Structured extraction & SC verification
  - **Level 3**: Accurate Pali rendering
  - **Level 4**: Commentary from my trusted teacher
  - **Level 5**: Soft, fun analogy that slowly converges to canon

**Example**:  
“Why am I anxious?” → mapped to feeling → craving → correct chain from Anguttara Nikaya

No generation of new ideas. No distortion.

---

## How the Path Works

- Starts simple with **Anguttara Nikaya** (Book of Ones, Twos, Threes…)
- Every chain you click becomes the starting point of **your personal path**
- Analogy begins short and light → gradually becomes richer
- Canon begins short → slowly grows deeper
- Progress is natural and driven by your own interaction

The system learns how different minds approach Dhamma over time.
